About Us

OUR CLINIC

Family Physiotherapy in Edmonton, AB is dedicated to comprehensive, compassionate care to our patients. From our clinic at 7408 139th Avenue (#G104) we begin each new client’s consultation with a thorough assessment followed by a recommended treatment plan. We include education and instruction components in the process to ensure that our clients can become partners in their own care by completing therapeutic exercises between visits.

OUR SERVICE

Our services include physiotherapy for orthopedic injuries, motor vehicle accident injuries, work related injuries under the Workers’ Compensation Board (WCB), and sports injuries under extended health insurance.

We also offer Massage Therapy, Acupuncture, Athletic Taping, Manual Therapy, Vestibular Rehabilitation, and Dry Needling.

OUR TEAM

Family Physiotherapy in Edmonton, AB brings an experienced, highly qualified team of Physiotherapists, Registered Massage Therapists, Support Staff and Registered Acupuncturist to help you accomplish your treatment goals and get back to your normal level of activity as quickly as possible.
